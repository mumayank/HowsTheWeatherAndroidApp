Weather, everybody wants to know how it is going to be during the week. Will it be rainy, windy, or sunny? This app helps you get the latest weather information of your favorite cities!

 

How to use

  • If you are using the app for the first time, please go to 'map' section from the navigation bar given at the bottom of the home screen. Select your favorite city by tapping it on the map. Confirm to bookmark it.
  • Once you have some cities bookmarked, you can easily select a city from the 'bookmarks' section of the home screen bottom navigation bar.
  • When you select a bookmarked city, you will go to the forecast screen. In this screen you will be able to see the latest weather updates of the city.
  • We have added some famous cities already in the app. You can easily search a city and look at its weather forecast.
